# spark


Welcome to the Spark STEM app, designed to ignite curiosity and encourage hands-on learning in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)! This app provides an engaging and interactive experience for children to explore and learn through challenges and experiments.

Features:
-Interactive Challenges: Fun and educational challenges in Math, Physics, and Chemistry.
-Video Lessons: Learn through engaging videos.
-Success Stories: Get inspired by real-world STEM success stories.
-Chemistry Experiments: Balance equations and perform virtual experiments.
-User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design for children to easily navigate.

How to Use:
-Explore the Challenges: Start by selecting a challenge from the home screen. Each challenge introduces fun, interactive lessons.
-Watch Videos: Tap the lesson cards to watch educational videos. Double-tap to skip ahead.
-Try the Experiments: In the Chemistry Lab, balance equations on the blackboard, then perform the experiment by clicking on the lab table.
